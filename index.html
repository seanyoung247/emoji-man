>> Game Rules <<



Welcome to Emoji-man, an emoji-based variation of the much-loved Pacman game which was born in the 1980s! We have included some interesting new themes. See the game rules below:



The main objectives are:

  • move Emoji-man around the maze using your arrow controls
  • collect points via food tokens and other collectables
  • avoid the baddies - they will drain your health or kill you!
  • try to escape the maze!


Themes

Each level has a different theme - which means different baddies, collectables and surprise characters...

Health

Watch your health level! Bumping into certain tokens will increase your health level... but bumping into baddies will decrease your health.
